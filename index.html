Rules/Instructions

    So this is how you play the game:

  1. You Start with 100 points, and you have three guesses to identify the city.
  2. As time decreases and you guess incorrectly, you will lose points.
  3. You also have three hints (Continent, Country and National Bird), but each hint results in a loss of points.
  4. You have 5 minutes, and the timer will start when you click the play now button.
  5. Good luck!
